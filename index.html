// About

Hey, I'm Julius.

I'm a Registered Electrical Engineer specialising in renewable energy and power systems modelling in the Philippines. I work with tools like DIgSILENT PowerFactory, ETAP, PVsyst, and pandapower to solve real grid problems.

Alongside the technical work, I write about the intersection of systems thinking, AI, productivity, and philosophy — the ideas that help engineers build careers, not just projects.

I run a Substack and create content on Threads and Instagram. My goal is simple: to be the bridge between deep technical expertise and clear, compelling communication for the next generation of Filipino engineers.
